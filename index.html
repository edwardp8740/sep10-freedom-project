Overview of Architecture


Architecture has existed for many years, in fact the exact origin of architecture can be placed back in the Neolithic period, about the year 10,000 B.C.E. Although architecture is mostly known as drawing buildings, architecture can actually also involve technology. This site will look into technological innovations in architecture for both the past and the possible future.

Here are old architecture drawings that were drawn in central Italy that date back to the 16th century, (1500s). The left drawing is a design for a wall tomb and the drawing on the right is architectural frame for altar.






Hardware tools for Architecture


Hardware tools for architecture are very handy on site. They can help make processes faster and more accurate. Some on site hardware tools are 3D Scanners and Laser Measurers. They're used to scan and upload accurate measured rooms. They're also other hardware tools that are used when drawing. For example, 3D pens, pen tablet, etc, they are all used for different reasons but they make the drawing process of architecture much more easier. VR can also be consider a hardware tool although it needs software applications to help run simulation of buildings and more.

Software Tools in Architecture


Software tools in architecture are used for multiple reasons. They could make 3D designs and overall more accurate and better visual drawings. There's a lot of software tools, all could be used for different reasons. Two software applications are chiefarchitect and autodesk. Both are used to make 3D designs and give a variety of options to buy for their system. They even teach people how to use the apps to their fullest. Another software app are VR simulations, they are used for more realistic 3D designs. It can give people a feel about how stuff will look like and be like.

Future Invention Idea



My Invention Idea


My idea consists of holographic projector for architecture. It will be a projector projecting 3rd holograms of buildings, rooms, etc.


Basic things this invention could help with or improve:

  • It will make architecture easier to visualize in 3d
  • It will let architects have an easier time designing buildings in real time.
  • It will also help the customer have their own opinion on design and other stuff.
  • It will be interactive meaning you can touch the hologram so you can move it around. This means you will be able to see the design of a building or room from different angles


Features of my Invention

The first feature of the projector will be that you can click to view one room only. You will be able to choose whether you want to see it as a whole or a single room on another screen or maybe a different part of the projector. When you choose to see a room alone, it will show everything that will be in the building like beds, furniture, etc.

The second feature will be that you can change the room’s design and even change furniture that you already have in the room. There would be a pre-design already in the machine and then a custom option where the person can make their own design/choose their own furniture.

The last feature will be that it can update or even make a separate copy from the original to the one the person would like. This will be used as a way for the architect showing the person as potential options of what they could have.
